The Problem

Tunisian founders are navigating without a clear path.

Existing guides, portals and chatbots give information — but they do not show the real project stage or the next best step.

Scattered information

Support programs, financing options and administrative steps are scattered across disconnected sources.

No clear project check

Founders do not have a clear way to know their real stage and main blockers.

Founder view vs. real stage

A project may seem funding-ready but still lack real customers or a clear revenue model.

Information exists. A clear path is missing.
The problem is not only lack of information. The real problem is lack of structured, personalized orientation for Tunisian entrepreneurs.
